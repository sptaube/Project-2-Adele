ADELE

ABOUT

Adele Laurie Blue Adkins was born on May 5, 1988, in Tottenham, London, and rose to fame with her soulful, emotive voice and emotionally charged songs. She graduated from the BRIT School for Performing Arts and released her debut album, 19, in 2008, which introduced her to the world. Her second album, 21 (2011), became one of the best-selling records in history, and her subsequent albums 25 (2015) and 30 (2021) have continued her commercial and critical success, earning her numerous awards, including multiple Grammys.

SOMEONE LIKE YOU

HELLO

Adele's "Weekends with Adele" residency was a Las Vegas show running from November 2022 to November 2024, known for its immersive production and intimate feel, despite being in a large venue. The show featured a 20-track setlist including major hits from 21 and 30, elaborate special effects like pyrotechnics and a waterfall, and a simple, consistent stage outfit for Adele. The residency was extended and concluded with a final run of shows in November 2024.
